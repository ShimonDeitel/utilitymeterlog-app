Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Utility Meter Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter (log entries and settings) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or entry contents. There are no accounts, no servers, and no network requests made by this app other than the App Store's own purchase and subscription verification, which is handled entirely by Apple.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed with it. We have no access to your data and cannot recover it.
